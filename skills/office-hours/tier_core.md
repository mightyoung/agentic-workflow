# OFFICE HOURS - Core Methodology

## Method Skeleton

- **Phase purpose**: Product consultation before coding — ask the right questions to understand what user really wants
- **Entry trigger**: User describes product idea or feature need, or requirements are unclear/fuzzy
- **Core method**: Expert simulation ("who understands this best?" — senior PM with 10+ years experience) + anti-pattern detection (over-optimization, speculation, scope creep, tech-driven, perfectionism, competitive anxiety) + structured Q&A (users, scenarios, success metrics, constraints, MVP) + optional Socratic deep-dive
- **Key principle**: Focus on user's real problem, not their proposed solution
- **Hard-gate exit**: Core product goal clarified, user confirms, MVP scope bounded, risks identified

## Iron Law

**SOLVE THE REAL PROBLEM, NOT THE PROPOSED SOLUTION**

Users often describe solutions. Your job: understand the underlying problem first, then help them find the best solution.
