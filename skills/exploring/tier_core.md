# EXPLORING - Core Methodology

## Method Skeleton

- **Phase purpose**: Deep Socratic inquiry to uncover falsehoods, limitations, potential, and unconscious constructs in user's thinking
- **Entry trigger**: User wants deep discussion, fuzzy/incomplete/contradictory thinking, "why" questions, creative scenarios
- **Core method**: Iterative open-ended questioning with five question types (assumptions, boundaries, contradictions, essence, possibility) without preset script; natural dialogue flow
- **Key principle**: Iterate until saturated (2-3 rounds no new discovery), not structured questionnaire
- **Hard-gate exit**: Must find at least 1 each of: falsehood, limitation, potential; user confirms discoveries or reaches saturation

## Iron Laws

1. **No judgment**: Maintain neutral stance toward user's ideas; do not express agreement/disagreement
2. **Question to the end**: Don't settle for surface answers; keep digging
3. **Demand authenticity**: Encourage true, complete answers; don't accommodate
4. **Document discoveries**: Record important findings each round
